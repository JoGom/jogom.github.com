THE DOORS

Biography

The Doors were an American rock band that formed in L.A. in the year of 1965
with vocalist Jim Morrison, keyboardist Ray Manzarek, guitarist Robby Krieger, and drummer John Densmore.
The Doors disbanded in 1973, just two years after Jim Morrison's death.

The band signed with Elektra Records in 1966 and during the time performed one of th most infuential rock acts of the time.
The group released eight albums in five years and had all but one go platinum or better.
